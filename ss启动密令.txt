CentOS7设置ss开机启动
配置自启动
新建启动脚本文件/etc/systemd/system/shadowsocks.service，内容如下：

[Unit]
Description=Shadowsocks
After=network.target
 
[Service]
TimeoutStartSec=0
ExecStart=/usr/bin/ssserver -c /etc/shadowsocks.json
 
[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target
